Community Guidelines

Last updated: December 2023

Membership

Eligibility

DriveTalk Community is open to current and aspiring UK delivery drivers. By joining, you confirm you're involved in or interested in the delivery industry.

Account Responsibility

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account and for all activities under your account.

Community Conduct

All members agree to:

  • Be respectful and professional in all communications
  • Share accurate and helpful information
  • Not spam other members with promotions
  • Respect others' privacy and confidentiality
  • Report any suspicious activity to administrators

Content Guidelines

Appropriate Content

Share driving tips, road updates, industry news, and professional advice. Keep discussions relevant to delivery driving in the UK.

Prohibited Content

No offensive language, personal attacks, illegal content, or commercial spam. Administrators may remove inappropriate content.

Disclaimer

Information Purpose: Content on this site is for informational purposes only. Always follow official UK highway codes and regulations.

Road Updates: While we strive for accuracy, road conditions change rapidly. Always verify closures with official sources before traveling.

Termination

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms or engage in harmful behavior toward the community.
